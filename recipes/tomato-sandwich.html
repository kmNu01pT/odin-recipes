Tomato sandwich recipe

Description

A sandwich that is so simple that anyone can make it. Very surprising how good it is and also made mayo a staple in other dishes that i make. Its surprisingly very good flavor being so simple.

Ingrediants

  • 2 slices of wheat bread (whatever bread you enjoy the most)
  • 1 beef steak tomato (perfectly ripe)
  • 1 jar of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of Toast and cover toast with mayo
  2. Cut tomato into 4-6 pieces and spread them evenly on each piece of bread
  3. sprinkle a bit of salt or your favorite cheese slice for some added flavor
  4. Enjoy!!!!
