Egg and Cheese Sandwich

Description

A marvelously simple dish you can whip up in about 10 minutes, taste amazing, and fill you up to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2 pieces of bread of choice (I use wheat bread)
  • 1 slice of Kraft American Cheese (ive noticed this is the best tasting one)
  • 1-2tsp of butter
  • 1 spatula

Steps

  1. turn on stove and preheat pan for a few minutes on low/medium heat (3-4 on dial)
  2. take the butter and spread it around the pan so the complete bottom is filled and sizziling slightly
  3. crack open the eggs and make sure they are spread around the pan (making sure you dont break the yolk)
  4. season with salt or pepper (optional)
  5. wait for 3-3:30 minutes or when the eggs stop bubbling on their sides
  6. take your spatula and flip them over making sure you dont break yolk (will not effect taste if yolk is broken just will look weird)
  7. put pieces of bread in toaster for 1-2 minutes (depending on how dark you like your toast)
  8. rip pieces of the kraft cheese in 2- 3 pieces and put them on the back of the eggs
  9. cover the pan with the lid to help the egg cook and also melt the cheese on top
  10. turn down heat to low (2-3 on dial) and leave it for 1 minute
  11. turn off heat and let it sit for 1 minute
  12. After remove lid and remove toast from toaster
  13. use spatula to put eggs on bread
